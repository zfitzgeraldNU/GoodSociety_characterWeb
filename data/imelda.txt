The dowager Imelda Hyde has let few things stand in the way of what she wants, including the untimely demise of her husband, the Baron Merrick Hyde. Her return to London is for the sake of her daughter, Esmerelda, who is entering her first season, as well as her son Edward who is entering his third season. Perhaps under her watchful eye and attentive gaze will they have luck finding loving (and wealthy) suitors for marriage.
