A pious man, Father Graham serves as the ton's resident priest from his pastoral estate in Bath. As no stranger to the drama and scandal, he is able to avoid becoming too entangled in the webs that this society weaves. Privy to most of its denizen's secrets, he works not to pass judgement but rather to guide them in the right direction. However, it remains to be seen how firm his faith really is and whether or not it will be tested.
