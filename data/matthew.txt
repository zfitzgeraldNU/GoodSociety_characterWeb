Recently returned from military service, Matthew possesses a dangerous wit and a weakness to the more hedonic aspects of life. Despite his time serving, he lives his day to day with abandon, as he is known to be an ardent gambler, and a flippantness that seems out of place for someone with his history. Most of society assumes him to be irresponsible or coping with his war wounds in the most lavish manner; however, he carries a deep sense of survivor's guilt that haunts him in his moments alone.
