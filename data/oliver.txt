A wealthy gentleman who returned unexpectedly from the Continent after the death of his wife. Reserved and difficult to read, Oliver rarely attends social functions and attracts endless speculation.

Rumors follow him wherever he goes. Some whisper that his wife died under suspicious circumstances. Others claim he was involved in espionage during the wars. Oliver never corrects any version of the story.

The truth is known only to him—and perhaps to the anonymous correspondent who has begun sending threatening letters.
