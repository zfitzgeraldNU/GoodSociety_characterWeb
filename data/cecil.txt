A rather unremarkable older gentleman, Duke Whitmore has retired primarily to his country estate in Bath, having found the bustle of London to be no longer to his taste. In his age, he has taken to becoming a general organizer of events and finds enjoyment in creating the situations for which the citizens of the Ton may escape from their lives temporarily. Mild mannered and largely indifferent to the minutia of Society, Duke Whitmore is a random insert NPC for me to help me fill in gaps when needed.
