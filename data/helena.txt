Sweet and unassuming , Helena has spent the last year taking care of her father as he passes. With her brother’s return as the head of the house and her mother’s quick remarriage (to Helena’s uncle no less) on the tip of everyone's tongue, Helena’s quiet reemergence into his society has been far less noticed. While Helena might not be the most dazzling in the room, if there is something you need, she is always the first to offer help. Helena dreams of finding a love match, someone who sees her first in any crowd, yet she understands that is a childish fantasy and is willing to settle for any match. Any at all.
